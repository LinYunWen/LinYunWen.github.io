## hyphen vs dash
- \- &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;hyphen: 用在兩個字的中間
    - 例如 dog-friendly restaurants
- – &nbsp;&nbsp;en dash: 用來取代 to
    - 例如 9-5 (9 to 5)
- — &nbsp;em dash: 用來分隔句子
    - 例如 Red, white, and blue—these are the colors of the flag
